WHY Universal?

The message is for the whole world. We not only urge people from India but also from every corner of the world to lend a helping hand and be a part of our mission. We live in a global village today. So India going up the rungs of the Human Development Index (HDI) ladder will definitely have a positive effect on the world on the whole. We are taking an initiative for a better India, which will be a part of a better world at large. It has also been used to signify that we will extend our helping hand to people, irrespective of any discrimination on the grounds of caste, creed, gender, region, religion, sect, etc.

WHY Live and Let Live?

The phrase “Live and Let Live” are very suggestive of our aim – we live and we help others live. Maybe fate has divided us on privileges but we can bridge the gap if we really have a mind for doing it. Being children of God, we are all equal. In the case of our NGO, we focus on providing two basic amenities of development in the world today – Education and Healthcare. Currently our focus is on education only.

WHY Mission?

The idea behind this word is very simple. We are on a “mission “– a mission where we desire to be a part of making India more enlightened, aware and healthy. Knowledge is power. We don’t want poverty or any other issue to be a hindrance in the path of education. As for healthcare, the availability of basic healthcare services is very poor in India. We will engage ourselves in various philanthropic activities in the healthcare sector at a later stage. Right now, our focus will be on education only.

THE beneficiaries?

No. Not only the members or other people involved with our activities, but the concept also extends to the beneficiaries in the future. We urge all of our past, present and future beneficiaries that once they stand on their own feet, they will help others establish themselves as successful individuals in life. They, along with everybody else will carry on the mission. This will be a never ending process.

Our Aim

Universal Live and Let Live Mission (ULLLM) was founded on 31st January 2009. It was registered on 28th May, 2009 under the West Bengal Societies Registration Act, 1961, vide Registration No. S/1L/60964, with the mission of helping poor students continue their education and also carry out other benevolent and philanthropic activities in the field of healthcare. Right now, we are focusing primarily on helping poor but brilliant students with their education. We aspire to dive into the healthcare sector a bit later.

The main objectives of ULLLM are as follows:

  • To help the brilliant but needy students of all communities, for pursuing their studies especially in medical science, engineering and other professional courses, by way of giving educational fees, books, hostel/accommodation charges and any other supports whatsoever.
  • To promote education among the indigent people by different means.
  • To open, acquire and run charitable medical centres, dispensary, hospitals, etc. for providing medical services to the helpless and other needy persons suffering from HIV- AIDS, cancer and any other diseases.
  • To arrange and organise medical awareness programmes of any kind.
  • To publish or cause to be published useful literatures, magazines pamphlets etc. relating to objects and activities of the Mission.

The other objects ULLLM are as follows:

  • To resort to all other activities that will be incidental and ancillary to the main objectives.
  • To engage in such other benevolent and philanthropic activities as may be deemed appropriate by the Governing Body.

How can you help?

Education and healthcare are two essential elements and indicators of development in the world today. But sadly, limited by fate, it is not equally attainable to everybody. So we strive for a better tomorrow for India and urge you all to join us in our endeavour. Today, we are limited in our approach due to lack of funds and subsequently, materials. But this can change with you joining us. Let’s not be a part of the change; let’s be the change!

There are several ways in which you can extend a generous helping hand. It not necessarily has to be financial. Here we are putting forward the ways you can associate yourself with us.

  • Membership
  • Donation
  • Volunteer
  • Sponsorship

Membership

We welcome you to be a part of our NGO by becoming a member. There are two membership schemes:

  • Yearly membership: You can become a member for a year at a time and it is not mandatory that you renew your membership for the next years. We would also hope you continue your membership in the subsequent years. We will be highly grateful to all of you joining us on our mission. The yearly membership fees are INR 300.
  • Life membership: We urge you all to invest in us your trust at the foremost. We promise that we will value your trust forever and not let you down. So join us for a lifetime with a one-time payment of INR 5000. We would always welcome your support forever and for always.

Donation

You can always donate in cash or in kind, even without being a member. We always tend to spend our money on things we don’t really need, so we might as well spend a little on something by which we can make someone’s life better. So we would urge you to spare a moment’s thought for those who are deprived of the basic facilities of Education and Healthcare. It’s a responsibility of all citizens of India to be responsible for a better tomorrow for India. In our initial phase, we are focusing on education only, so your generous contributions will right now be utilized in helping students continue their education. All other donations in kind should be pertaining to education only as of now.

We believe personal efforts add up to a magnanimous result and transforms into a sign of hope and positivity for everybody. So we earnestly request you to trust us with your generous contributions and we promise not a single penny or the generous thought behind such contributions will be disappointed. We are looking forward to your help with optimism.

Volunteer

It’s not only your membership and donations that we value. We value your involvement too. This again is beyond memberships. You can volunteer in various activities of our NGO. There will be various awareness campaigns and various other activities that you can be a part of, without paying any money at all. We will call for volunteers as and when we would require people to help us along with our activities. The criteria will be set according to the needs and demands of the programs. This will be in accordance with the decision of the Governing body of our NGO. Their decision will be final and binding. We hope that you come forward to help us whole-heartedly when we require your services.

Sponsorship

Besides all these, we welcome sponsorships in any kind. You have options in here too. You can sponsor any of our activities, our two annual programmes viz. Induction Ceremony and Foundation Day Celebrations, any student with his/her education as of now, or in any other way you may deem fit. You `will get your due promotion on our site by our acknowledging you on our homepage.

We work for those who deserve a bright future and social recognition. But sadly there is not a lot we can do alone, so our motive is to join hands together and work as a team to make it count. The concept:

It often happens that after a day’s work, we come home and see maybe a rupee or two in our pocket. Not much at all. And in present days’ worth, it really has no value. Maybe it fulfils a child’s wish of a chocolate or maybe helps us in daily transactions like in shops or auto-rickshaws or bus fares. But in layman terms, they are worth nothing.

Wrong!

Here we present before you all a novel idea. We say that every rupee matters. Let us think logically. If we are left with a rupee a day, that makes INR 365 a year. Now that’s some amount at least, which is still less considered on a yearly basis. In fact we spend on our luxury that costs us way above this price.

We urge each and every one, be it members, volunteers, or anybody else, to save, at least a rupee a day. It can be on behalf of the family as a whole or each person of the family on his/her own. We urge you all to save it for the greater purpose of education for all and for a healthier India, which our NGO aims to propagate and support whole-heartedly. Even your little contributions like this will make a difference at large when all of us will maintain a “piggy bank” and donate its amount to our cause. Again, it extends beyond the purview of membership.

Types:

There are four types of piggy banks one can subscribe for :

  1. Monthly
  2. Quarterly
  3. Half-Yearly
  4. Annually

Monthly: For those subscribing for this scheme, we will collect the amount of your piggy bank after every month.

Quarterly: For those subscribing for this scheme, we will collect the amount of your piggy bank after every 3 months.

Half-yearly: For those subscribing for this scheme, we will collect the amount of your piggy bank after every 6 months.

Annually: For those subscribing for this scheme, we will collect the amount of your piggy bank after every year.
